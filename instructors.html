Instructors of BODY TUNE...

Gym instructors are fitness professionals who provide guidance and support to individuals in achieving their fitness goals. They work in gyms, health clubs, and fitness centers, and are responsible for designing and implementing exercise programs for clients based on their fitness level, goals, and needs. Gym instructors typically have knowledge of various exercise techniques and equipment, and are able to teach clients how to use them safely and effectively. They may also provide advice on nutrition and lifestyle changes to support overall health and wellness.
